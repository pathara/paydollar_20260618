VoicePay

Innovative Payment Solution Enabling
Secure, Contactless Voice-Activated Transactions

What is VoicePay?

AsiaPay VoicePay is an innovative payment solution that leverages voice recognition to enable secure, contactless transactions. Designed for convenience and accessibility, VoicePay allows users to complete transactions simply by speaking to a device, such as a smart speaker or smartphone—eliminating the need for physical cards, keypads, or manual input.

How It Works

A seamless 4-step voice payment journey

1. Initiate

The user speaks a payment instruction into their device.

2. Process

System processes the command via AI and voice recognition.

3. Secure

Payment is authenticated through the selected eWallet.

4. Confirm

The user receives instant confirmation of the transaction.
