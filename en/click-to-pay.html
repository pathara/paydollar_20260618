Click to Pay

No Passwords. New Checkout Experience
Click to Pay revolutionizes online checkout by combining card tokenized-leading security with a seamless, one-click payment experience. Powered by Visa and Mastercard's EMV® Secure Remote Commerce (SRC) standard, this innovative solution eliminates the need for customers to manually enter payment details, significantly reducing checkout friction while maintaining the highest levels of card payment.

How Click to Pay Works:

📝 Easy Enrollment: Customers securely store their credit card details at digital token once during their first purchase.
🔒 Tokenized Security: Payment information is replaced with a unique digital token, keeping sensitive data protected.
⚡ Instant Checkout: Returning customers simply click to pay without re-entering credentials.
✅ Universal Acceptance: Works across devices, browsers, and participating merchant sites.

Key Benefits for E-Commerce Merchants:


Boost Conversions
Reduce cart abandonment by up to 70% with faster checkout experiences.

Enhance Security
Advanced tokenization and biometric authentication with EMV 3-D Secure (3DS) minimize fraud risks.

Increase Customer Loyalty
Streamlined payments encourage repeat purchases.

Simplified Compliance
Reduce PCI DSS scope by eliminating storage of sensitive payment data.

As consumer expectations for fast, secure digital payments continue to rise, Click to Pay provides merchants with a competitive advantage by delivering both convenience and protection. The solution integrates seamlessly with existing payment infrastructures, requiring minimal technical adjustments while offering maximum security benefits.

Early adopting merchants report significant improvements in checkout completion rates and customer satisfaction scores, making Click to Pay an essential tool for any business looking to optimize their e-commerce performance in today's digital marketplace.
