Security Policy

Online security is vital to our core business. We fully understand your security concerns and we are committed to taking whatever measures are necessary to protect you, your online data, and your transactions.

PayDollar uses the internationally accredited security technologies and measures to provide you with total peace of mind whenever you use our service(s).

Data Encryption
We use the highest commercially available encryption technology - 256-bit SSL encryption via VeriSign's Global Site Certificate technology. All confidential information is automatically encrypted before it is transmitted, to protect the data from being read and interpreted. To learn more, click VeriSign's Global Site Certificates.

We also use SET technology, jointly developed by VISA and MasterCard to secure payment transactions over open networks, to protect credit card data transmissions between our system and that of our banking partner(s) - thereby ensuring the utmost security [for all data transmissions].

System Protection
All customer and transaction information is stored in servers that are well protected, both physically and electronically.

Access Control
You can only access the service with your registered e-mail address and PayDollar password. To protect your account from any unauthorized access, e.g., if you forget to logoff from PayDollar after finishing a session, we will automatically deactivate your account if your PayDollar session is inactive for more than ten minutes. And, we will automatically suspend your membership if five invalid password attempts are made in any one session.

e-mail Notification
We send payment to confirmed e-mail addresses only. We also send notification messages via e-mail to your e-mail box for every transaction you have registered in the system, so you are always fully advised of all activity relating to your account.

Customer Authentication
We take various appropriate authentication measures to validate and ensure the legitimacy of the authorized account holder.

Please refer to the FAQ about other service details and Terms of Use.
