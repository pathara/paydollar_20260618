InsertPass

Digital Pass Management Enabling
Secure Post-Purchase Pass Storage

What is InsertPass?

InsertPass is a value-added digital wallet feature designed to streamline the post-payment experience for shoppers after checkout, while empowering merchants with enhanced engagement and operational efficiency. It enables users to store, share, and manage loyalty cards, tickets, and coupons directly in their iOS and Android wallets after payment.

Why it matters to business?

Enhanced Customer Experience

Offers a frictionless way to manage multiple purchased passes and tickets in one mobile wallet, allowing users to seamlessly store access and redeem loyalty cards, tickets, and coupons together.

Easy Setup

API-based integration with AsiaPay’s payment gateway ensures quick deployment without major development or migration.

Operational Efficiency

Digital passes reduce printing and distribution costs, streamline campaign management, and simplify redemption processes.

Brand Visibility

Merchants’ logos and offers remain visible in customers’ wallets, reinforcing brand presence and loyalty.

Scalability & Flexibility

Suitable for businesses of all sizes, with customizable branding options to align with merchant identity.

What’s more about InsertPass?

InsertPass is a future-ready e-voucher solution designed to simplify digital engagement as a complete tool solution. By combining e-voucher distribution, CRM integration, and mobile wallet compatibility. InsertPass empowers businesses to strengthen loyalty, drive repeat purchases, and stay competitive in a mobile-first marketplace.
