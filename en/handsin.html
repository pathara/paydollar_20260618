Split Payment

Split payments across cards and groups

AsiaPay is proud to partner with Hands In, the world's leading split payment provider, to help merchants boost conversions and reduce payment friction.

This collaboration lets AsiaPay merchants offer customers the flexibility to split payments across multiple cards or among group members in a single checkout — improving satisfaction, reducing cart abandonment, and driving incremental revenue.

Why It Matters for Merchants

Generate High-value Payment
With Hands In, customers can divide payments across multiple credit cards, helping you capture transactions that might otherwise fail due to insufficient funds. This means fewer declined payments and higher overall sales conversion.
Make Group Payments Easier
For travel, hospitality, ticketing, and luxury retail businesses, Hands In makes group payments effortless. Each participant can pay their share via QR code, payment link, text, or email — reducing administrative hassle and enhancing the booking experience.
Increase Average Order Value (AOV)
By giving customers flexible payment options, merchants can encourage higher-value purchases and group bookings, driving incremental growth and long-term loyalty.
Seamless Integration & Management
Hands In integrates directly into AsiaPay’s secure payment gateway, allowing you to receive one consolidated payment while the system automatically handles all individual transactions behind the scenes.

How It Works:

1. Customer Checks Out

At your online checkout, the customer selects “Split Payment”.

2. Flexible Payment Options

They can split the total by amount, percentage, or evenly among cards or group members.
