Visa Passkey

Faster, safer, and more inclusive
authentication for digital payments

Introduction to Visa Payment Passkey

Visa Payment Passkey (VPP) is an advanced authentication solution for card payment. It replaces traditional passwords and one-time passcodes (OTPs) with secure, device-based biometric verification. Built on the Fast Identity Online (FIDO) international standards, it allows Visa cardholders to authenticate payments using their device's built-in features—such as facial recognition, fingerprint scanning, or device PIN.

Faster, safer, and more inclusive authentication for digital payments. With passkeys single registration, consumers can complete transactions with a streamlined authentication experience, eliminating the friction of typing passwords or waiting for SMS codes that can be delayed or intercepted.

The Visa Payment Passkey is a next-generation solution for card-not-present (CNP) authentication, offering a phishing-resistant alternative that significantly enhances security while improving the checkout experience.

✨Key features


Visa Payment Passkey is designed as a versatile solution that functions seamlessly across the entire digital payment ecosystem.

Integration with Visa's Payment Stack

Visa Payment Passkey is deeply integrated with Visa's existing payment infrastructure:

🔐
Tokenization

The passkey framework combines Visa Token Service (VTS) with FIDO authentication tied to consumer devices. This dual-layered approach enhances security while enabling seamless transactions.

👆
Click to Pay Integration

Providing consistent checkout experience across participating merchants.

👤
Guest Checkout

The solution works with VPP Direct and Cardinal Consumer Authentication to enable passkey-based guest checkout without requiring a full account creation.

Support for All Transaction Types

Visa Payment Passkey supports authentication across:


💳 Credit and debit card transactions 
🛍️ E-commerce purchases at participating online merchants
📱 In-app payments and digital wallet transactions
🔄 Recurring payments and subscription billing (when combined with Card Account Updater services) 
Support both IOS and Android devices

What’s more Benefits for Merchants

Visa Payment Passkey delivers competitive advantages for merchants seeking to optimize the shopper's checkout journey:

Higher Conversion Rates

Minimizing the friction caused by OTP-based authentication reduces transaction failures and abandoned carts.

Faster Checkout Times

Passkeys dramatically reduce checkout duration, increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Fraud Reduction

Passkeys are inherently phishing-resistant. Unlike OTPs, which can be stolen through social engineering, passkeys use cryptographic keys stored locally on the user's device.

Simplified Integration

Merchants can access passkey functionality through their existing Payment Service Provider (PSP)—no complex infrastructure rebuilds are required.

Authentication Program Qualification

By implementing passkeys, merchants may qualify for Visa’s authentication programs, potentially reducing liability and optimizing interchange costs.
