What is AppPay?

AppPay is our latest user-friendly application for accepting retail payments. With AppPay, merchants can easily accept real-time payments using their smartphone or smart payment terminal via cards or QR codes.

Designed for your needs

Accept Payments on the Go

Our payment service packages are designed to help you choose the best solution for accepting retail payments.

SmartPOS

Manage all your payments in a single terminal.
Compatible with the latest smart terminal devices, such as SUNMI, NEXGO, PAX, LANDI and more.

SoftPOS

Transform your smart device into a payment terminal.
No extra hardware needed.

Support Your Customers To Pay In Multiple Ways

Cards -
Tap to Pay

Merchants enter the amount to be collected, and customers tap their cards to pay.

QR Payments -
Customer-Presented Mode

Customers display their QR codes on their smart mobile devices, and the merchant scans the QR code to collect payments.

QR Payments -
Merchant-Presented Mode

Merchants generate a QR code and present it to the customer for scanning to collect payments.

Payment Link or QR Code

Merchants generate a payment link and QR code to share via email and instant messengers with customers for collecting payments.

AppPay Terminal QR Code

Customers scan the AppPay Terminal code (which contains Merchant ID and Terminal ID) to make payments on the PayDollar Payment Gateway.
